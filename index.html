The Life-Changing Magic of Tidying Up

The Japanese Art of Decluttering and Organizing

By Marie Kondo


The reason that I chose this book is because while working on my Ph.D I noticed that I had so much stuff lying around. I would always forget what I purchased, and therefore purchase another of the same item. Before I knew what happened, I had four bottles of dishwashing soap, three boxes of Hefty trashbags...and I can't even count how many boxes of Q-tips I had. (I think it took me about two years to get through them all.)
Back to the book. I wanted to find a way to organize my little two bedroom apartment. It was hard to do though. I was definitely in that mindset of "Well, I should keep it because I might need it one day."
